T.A.L.O.N. Community Safety UI Proposal

Static wireframes for the community safety plan. These screens keep the existing desktop shell pattern while adding patrols, protective details, assignment status, check-ins, duress, incident reporting, and harm-reduction support. The proposal avoids suspect or person-of-interest tracking and keeps sensitive vulnerable-person details minimal until core-level privacy controls exist.

Approval choices

  • Add a new Assignments board as the command-post home for these workflows.
  • Extend Missions for patrol/detail setup before creating dedicated patrol tables.
  • Add Incidents as structured event reports linked to missions, SITREPs, assets, documents, and follow-up tasks.
  • Treat the mobile screen as future direction after desktop/core workflow approval.

Mobile approval notes

  • Map-first screen with one-tap check-in and duress actions.
  • Current assignment card shows only what a field operator needs immediately.
  • Offline state and pending sync stay visible without blocking field use.
  • Quick incident and SITREP capture can reuse current map context.
  • Enrollment bundle, offline map packages, and metadata stripping remain separate implementation decisions.
